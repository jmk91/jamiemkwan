This is a journal of doodles that capture a year of life for interdisciplinary designers, Zeynep Sen & Jamie Kwan, as recorded in the moments of data that make up our lives. These are the stories between the lines of data; stories of living, of loving, of learning, and of letting go.

For a year, we pledged to record and exchange data that made up a single week, resulting in 52 challenges each, in creatively visualizing our lives. We did this all in an effort to learn about data visualization, exercising sketching-just-for-fun, and in self-reflecting on our personal lives; drawing inspiration from Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec's award-winning project, Dear Data.

Some of these weeks include:

  1. A Week of Coffee;
  2. A Week of Optimism;
  3. A Week of Distractions;
  4. A Week of Thank Yous;
  5. A Week of Family.
