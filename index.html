My Story

DESIGNER | STRATEGIST | STORYTELLER

I believe that great experiences inspire play and curiosity.

Armed with the tools of both an architect and a digital strategist, I am a passionate brand storyteller who is dedicated to crafting engaging human experiences in the real world. By seeing branded storytelling from both a spatial and a pixel scale, these crafted experiences prompt a strong sense of engagement through a holistic approach. I believe in applying the elements of storytelling and play to build stronger relationships through brand design in the real-world.
